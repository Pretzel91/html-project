With an innovative toucj to the recipes that has been passed down from generatioms, we labor to deliver a scrumptious experience in every bite. With Makkuse we aim to revive the lost recipes and make the Nepali richness well known across the globe whilst meeting the expectactions of customers, making sure we use locally resourced raw products by sourcing the local talent.
